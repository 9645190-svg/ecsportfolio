My interest is in music, but especially playing the guitar and singing. I have been playing for about 4 years now and am still learning so much. I am the only one in my family that plays guitar and sings. I make an effort to practice daily and have been moving on to learning advanced songs that require lead guitar picking styles and the more excessive use of bar chords.

Timeline:

2021-I started playing guitar. I started off playing acoustic, and learning simple songs.

June 8, 2023- I preformed in front of a live audience for the first time. I was at a camp called FSY, and they were holding a show during that week to show off talents and skills, which I decided to enter playing guitar while singing. I preformed the popular song Riptide, sung by Vance Joy.

November 13, 2023- I preformed my second time at a talent show for my middle school, Olympus Junior High. I decided on playing Roar by Katy Perry.
